Please click below to hear experiences of few participants, who attended the Bhava Spandana program.

Sadhguru has often spoken about how Bhava Spandana can be an important turning point in one’s spiritual journey and many years of regular sadhana can be covered in just these three and half days of this programme.

That’s why, we offer this programme in various cities besides Isha Yoga Centre. No matter where it is conducted, through Sadhguru’s grace, an intensely energetic situation is created, where individuality and the limitations of the five sense organs can be transcended, creating an experience of oneness and resonance with the rest of existence.
