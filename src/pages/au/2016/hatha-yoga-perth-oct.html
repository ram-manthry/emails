We are happy to announce that we will be offering Surya Kriya & Angamardana programs in Perth!

Isha Hatha Yoga programs are an unparalleled opportunity to learn practices derived from a yogic tradition maintained in its full purity and vibrancy for thousands of years. The classes will be conducted by Isha Hatha Yoga school trained teachers and will offer classical Hatha Yoga in its full depth and dimension.

In this article, Sadhguru introduces us to the phenomenal science of hatha yoga, corrects many misunderstandings about the process and explores how we can reap the maximum benefits hatha yoga has to offer.

{{> drop-shadow }} Hatha Yoga Programs {{> spacer-white}}

Following programs are being offered during the weekend of 15 - 16 October.

Surya Kriya - Fire up the Sun Within

Surya Kriya is a potent yogic practice of tremendous antiquity, designed as a holistic process for health, wellness and complete inner wellbeing.

Date: Sat 15th Oct, 8am - 11am & 4pm - 7pm
(Both sessions are mandatory)

Angamardana - The Ultimate Fitness Session

Angamardana is a system rooted in yoga, that offers to everyone the opportunity to invigorate the body and to reach peak physical and mental health.

Date: Sun 16th Oct, 7:30am - 11:00am & 4pm - 7:30pm
(Both sessions are mandatory)

Surya Kriya Refresher

If you have already learned Surya Kriya, there is a chance to

  • go through the practice guided
  • get your practices corrected
  • deepen the experience of your practice
  • restart the practice if you have discontinued it, and clarify any doubts that you may have

Date: Sat, Oct 15th - 1pm to 3pm

Venue: Willetton Senior High School, Pinetree Gully Road, Willetton 6156

For any further information or clarification, you can email australia@ishafoundation.org or call 0469 560 239.
