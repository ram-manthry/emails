Dear {{{{raw}}}}{{FIRST_NAME}}{{{{/raw}}}},

We eagerly await your participation in the Bhava Spandana Programme from the 8th to the 11th of October 2016. Please find below some important information regarding your participation in the program in addition to what was sent earlier to you (copied once again below for your reference).

We want to remind you that as a part of the programme you are required to complete the homework assignment.

You need to spend a minimum of 5 hours (need not be continuous) writing this homework in a separate notebook designated just for this purpose. Please note that it is not maximum of 5 hours, it is a minimum of 5 hours. How deeply you involve yourself in doing this homework will determine the depth of your inner experience. So please invest sufficient amount of time into it.

This homework is for you. Nobody is going to read it, neither the teacher nor the volunteers. It is only for your eyes only.

Emergency Contact Number {{> spacer-white}}

To further support the inward process that this programme offers, you will not have the opportunity to use your mobile phone or laptop etc., to communicate with friends or family during your stay at the PGL Campaspe Downs Country Resort. So if you want you can give them the following mobile number for them to use in case of an emergency only: 0477 388 593.

Registration will start at 1:00 pm on 8th October. {{> spacer-white}}

Please note that you need to plan your breakfast or/and lunch in such a way that you are on empty stomach at 5.30 pm on Saturday, 8th October, which means your full meal should be over by 1:30 pm. Please note that lunch will not be provided at the venue due to this requirement.

Your accommodation at the PGL Campaspe Downs will be in shared rooms. Bathrooms and toilets will also be shared.

Please carry the following items to the program {{> spacer-white}}
  • Completed homework assignment with pen.Please note if you wrote your homework digitally on your mobile or tablet device, please bring a printout of this homework along with you.
  • Comfortable clothing suitable for physical exercises and sitting on the floor (at least 6 sets of clothes)
  • Warm jacket or sweater and rain-proof jacket. Ensure you have sufficient warm clothing since the weather can fluctuate during the month of October. Be prepared for wet weather and cold nights.
  • Comfortable and warm clothing, enough for 4 days
  • Sleeping bag (suitable for low temperatures in range of 5 degree celsius) and warm enough blankets. (Pillow and fitted sheet will be provided with mattress). To check the weather predictions for the venue during those dates, please click here.
  • Torch, personal items (towel, toiletries, ...), umbrella
  • Medication, in case you take any plus any medication you may need.
  • Trainers or walking shoes, Bathroom Slippers
  • Insect repellent
  • Yoga mat and Cushions (if needed) to sit on the floor and for the practices.
  • Flask (if needed) for hot drinking water?

We look forward to having you with us.

Sincerely,
Isha Australia Volunteers
