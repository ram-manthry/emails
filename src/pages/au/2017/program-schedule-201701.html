{{> drop-shadow }} Angamardana - The Ultimate Yogic Workout {{> spacer-white-small }}

Angamardana means gaining complete mastery over the limbs, organs and other parts of the body. This practice is believed to be a pre-cursor of various martial arts. It creates aerobic exercise and muscle tone without bringing rigidity into the body.

  • Strengthens the spine, skeletal and muscular systems
  • Builds physical strength and fitness
  • Invigorates the body, bringing a sense of lightness and freedom
  • Prepares the body for Hatha Yoga
