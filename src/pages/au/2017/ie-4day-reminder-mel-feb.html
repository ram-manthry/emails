 {{> drop-shadow }} Empty Stomach Condition

Before doing your practices, it is important that you are on empty stomach.

Below are the guidelines for the different types of food and the time gap required before starting practices.

  • 4 hours gap after a full meal
  • 2.5 hours gap after a snack (a fruit or a biscuit)
  • 1.5 hours gap after a beverage (tea, coffee or milk)
  • 45 mins to 1 hour gap after a cigarette
  • 10 hours gap after consuming alcohol
