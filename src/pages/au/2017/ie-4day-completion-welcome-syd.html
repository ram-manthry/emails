{{> drop-shadow }} Monthly Group Meditation (Sathsang) {{> spacer-white-small }}

We conduct monthly group meditation sessions for those initiated into Shambhavi. It is an opportunity to deepen your kriya with Sadhguru guiding you through the Shambhavi practice. You can also receive corrections for your kriya by trained Isha volunteers and enjoy powerful guided meditations and exclusive videos screened at the sessions. Please ensure to be on empty stomach. We close doors at 5.45PM sharp to ensure other meditators are not disturbed, so please ensure you are on time.
