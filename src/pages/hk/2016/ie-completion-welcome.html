{{> drop-shadow }} Isha Kriya Programs {{> spacer-white-small }}

Isha Kriya is a powerful 12-minute meditation process designed by Sadhguru for anyone seeking to instill a drop of spirituality in their lives. It is free, simple, and easy to practice. Available as a guided meditation with an instructional video and downloadable instructions, it has the potential to transform the life of anyone who is willing to invest just a few minutes a day.
