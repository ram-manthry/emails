Namaskaram,

We have just 18 days remaining before he will be with us and let us leave aside all our inhibitions and offer this to everyone around us!

    {{> drop-shadow }} Share your experience with Sadhguru {{> spacer-white-small }}

    If knowing Sadhguru has transformed your life in some way, it is important that we share our experience with others! Isn't this of ultimate significance in bringing transformation to other life?

    Gaurav shares his experience with Sadhguru:

    “ My first physical encounter with him was at a evening sathsang in Ashram. The first sentence he uttered completely blew my mind. It was exactly something I was always running from but at the same time always knew to be true. He spoke about how if we don't go beyond the limitations of our mind we can't know life in its entirety. Mind is the only thing I had worked on till then, and I had seen it's limitations. After that he answered many questions people asked but it felt like everything was being said to me personally.

    I secretly thought that this is all a setup to get me here and tell me all these things I needed to hear. I wondered how he knew me so well. I wondered if he ever came to Chandigarh when I was a young boy and saw me and knew exactly who I was.

    Then again I saw him at ashram at one more evening sathsang and while leaving he just looked at me once. It was just a glance, but I felt like he knew exactly who I am. Like he knew much more about me than myself. It's hard to forget the intensity in his eyes. Hard to forget and hard to explain. Since then I've seen him only a few times in person but the intimacy of this bond has grown beautifully with time. What started as a pure intellectual pursuit, perhaps an intellectual challenge, has now evolved into a loving long distance relationship. In any normal circumstances one could call it one sided love affair. But with Sadhguru, it would be a big mistake to call it one sided.”
