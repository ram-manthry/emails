Date & Time: 12 November, 5:00 pm to 7:15 pm
Venue: Next Step Asia, 2/F, Woo Shing Kee Industrial Building, No 138 Wai Yip St, Kwun Tong ( Map)

Inner engineering online participants
Please be outside the door not later than 35 minutes of start time to join the session after the Shambhavi practice.

* Please note that Sathsang will be conducted on the 2nd Saturday of every month across all Asia Pacific centers including Hong Kong starting from Jan 2017.
