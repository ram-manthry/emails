Greetings!

With only three days to go for the Inner Engineering Total program, here is some quick info for your reference.

On Thursday Mar 23, Registrations open from 5:30pm and program starts at 7pm. Please make sure to arrive at least 30 minutes before to allow time for registration and settling down. Also include additional time to cater for traffic delays and parking.

You can also bring along your family and friends (anyone over the age of 15) to accompany you for first part of the session on 23rd March. No registration is required. They can simply walk-in. Post this session, if they wish they may register for the program if places are still available.


Program Details

Session Timings:

  • Thu 23 Mar: 7:00 PM to 10:15 PM
  • Fri 24 Mar: 7:00 PM to 10:15 PM
  • Sat 25 Mar: 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM*
  • Sun 26 Mar: 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM*

* Please note the change in closing times on Sat & Sun

Venue: Sandilands Center [MAP]
Address: The Hong Kong Girl Guides Association - Sandilands Center, 141, Wong Nei Chung Gap Road

Please note that:
  • Attendance for all the sessions is mandatory.
  • Doors will close at the session start time and latecomers will be not be allowed inside the hall.

Transport Assistance

  • A bus pickup has been arranged at cost basis for those of you who wish to avail this. The pickup and drop-off point will be from Causeway Bay.
  • There is no free parking available at the venue. A paid parking at Hong Kong Cricket Club might be available close to the venue. It is 30 HKD per hour.

Additional Information

  • Lunch will be provided on Saturday.
  • Breakfast and lunch will be provided on Sunday.
  • Please wear loose comfortable clothing and bring a yoga mat or a cushion to sit comfortably on the floor.
