With only three days to go for the Inner Engineering Total program, here is some quick information for your reference.

  • Thursday, 23 March: 7 pm to 10:15 pm
  • Friday, 24 March: 7 pm to 10:15 pm

  • Sunday, 25 March: 7 am to 7 pm*

Contact: Gina (5363 5216). Note: bus schedule for subsequent sessions will be provided at Venue.
