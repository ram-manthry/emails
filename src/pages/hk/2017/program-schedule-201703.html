March 2017 {{> drop-shadow }} Surya Kriya - Fire up the Sun within {{> spacer-white-small }}

Surya Kriya is to activate the internal sun, but you stimulate it by using the external sun.

- Sadhguru

{{> spacer-white-small }}

Surya Kriya, the forefather to Surya Namaskar (Sun Salutation) is a powerful process of activating the sun within you. It balances a person’s energies, leading to stability of the body and stillness of the mind. This strong foundation becomes the basis to explore higher dimensions of life.

  • Develops mental clarity and focus
  • Remedies weak constitutions
  • Balances hormonal levels in the body
  • Prepares one for deeper states of meditation

{{> drop-shadow }} Bhuta Shuddhi {{> spacer-white-small }}

The basis of all creation, including the physical body, is the five elements - earth, water, wind, fire and space. The wellbeing of the body and mind can be established by purifying these five elements within the human system.
  • Keeps the system in harmony and balance
  • Prepares the system to handle powerful states of energy
  • Enhances the capabilities of the physical body, mind and energy
  • Creates the basis to gain complete mastery over the human system
Date & Time: 9 Apr (Sun) : 5 pm to 6:30 pm
Venue: Club House, Estoril Court, 55 Garden Road, (Map) Central, HK Island

{{> drop-shadow }} Isha Kriya {{> spacer-white-small }}

“Isha” refers to that which is the source of creation; “kriya” literally means “internal action”. The purpose of Isha Kriya is to help an individual get in touch with the source of his existence, to create life according to his own wish and vision. Daily practice of Isha Kriya brings health, dynamism, peace and wellbeing. It is a powerful tool to cope with the hectic pace of modern life.

  • Enhanced Focus and Clarity
  • Improved Health
  • Increased Energy
  • Greater sense of peace & joy
Date & Time: 9 Apr (Sun) : 2:30 pm to 3:30 pm
Venue: Club House, Estoril Court, 55 Garden Road, (Map) Central, HK Island
