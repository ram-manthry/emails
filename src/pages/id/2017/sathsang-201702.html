{{> drop-shadow }} IYC Retreat 24th - 30th March 2017 {{> spacer-white }}

We take many holidays in our lives, be it for shopping, relaxation or to simply get away….Once in a while comes an opportunity for a retreat that nourishes the soul. One such a trip happened in July 2016 to the Isha Yoga Centre, Coimbatore.

What is the Isha Yoga Centre Tour about?

  • A 5 nights stay in a consecrated space of Isha Yoga Centre, Coimbatore.
  • Lodging arranged in the standard AC cottages on shared basis at the Isha Yoga Centre.
  • Wholesome vegetarian meals at the Yoga Centre.
  • Guided Isha Yoga Centre Tour by trained residents.
  • Dedicated space and trained teachers guiding the daily sadhana.
  • A chance to participate in the ‘Pancha Bhuta Aradhana’ offering at the Dhyanalinga.
  • A volunteer accompanying the group from Singapore airport and back.
