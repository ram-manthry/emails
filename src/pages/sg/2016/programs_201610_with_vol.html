October, 2016 {{> drop-shadow }} {{> spacer-white-small }}

A walk amidst nature, with chirping birds, green trees and 'just rising' sun for company... Come nourish your soul.
Get your walking shoes and join us along with your family for a walk on Saturday morning, 22nd Oct. The walk will be followed by Shambhavi Mahamudra practice at the lush Fort Canning Park. After the practice we will head for breakfast at Tank Road Temple. Volunteer meet will be held after the breakfast.

Schedule:

  • 7.30am sharp: Meet at Promenade MRT Stn Exit A for the walk.
  • 8.30am: Shambhavi Mahamudra practice at Fort Canning Park (Assemble near Fort Gate).
  • 9.30am: Breakfast at Sri Thendayuthapani Temple
  • 10.30am: Volunteers' Meet at Sri Thendayuthapani Temple
    (15, Tank Road, Singapore 238065)

Things to bring: A yoga mat, water bottle and a lively spirited you!

Most of you might already know that Isha Yoga is run entirely by volunteers. On many occassions Sadhguru has spoken how volunteering is an essential part of one's inner growth. To read more, please click here.

It was highly motivating to see a large number of new volunteers turning out for September volunteer meet. Many of you submitted the forms to register your preference to volunteer for various activities in the upcoming programmes as well as January event with Sadhguru. For those who could not make it in September but you would like to volunteer regularly, you can register your interest as a volunteer here.

The upcoming Volunteer Meet on Oct 22 will focus on Sadhguru's January event besides regular Isha programs and is mandatory for all who wish to volunteer for this event.
