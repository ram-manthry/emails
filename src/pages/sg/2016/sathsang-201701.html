{{> drop-shadow }} {{> drop-shadow }} Mahashivratri Sadhana and Yoga Veera Sadhana {{> spacer-white }}

We will be guiding you through the various aspects of the Mahashivratri Sadhana designed by Sadhguru as a preparation for this year's Mahashivratri – a night of tremendous possibilities.

We will also be giving details about Yoga Veera Sadhana during this month's sathsang. This is a possibility opened up for all volunteers by Sadhguru.

It is a sadhana of 40/21/12/7/3 days duration preceding Mahashivratri where volunteers are given sadhana to do daily in front of Adi Yogi and dedicate themselves to Adi Yogi.

 {{> drop-shadow }} IYC Retreat 24th - 30th March 2017 {{> spacer-white }}

We take many holidays in our lives, be it for shopping, relaxation or to simply get away….Once in a while comes an opportunity for a retreat that nourishes yourself. One such trip happened in July 2016 to the Isha Yoga Centre, Coimbatore.

What is the Isha Yoga Centre Tour about?

  • A 5 nights stay in a consecrated space of Isha Yoga Centre, Coimbatore.
  • Lodging arranged in the standard AC cottages on shared basis at the Isha Yoga Centre.
  • Wholesome vegetarian meals at the Yoga Centre.
  • Guided Isha Yoga Centre Tour by trained residents.
  • Dedicated space and trained teachers guiding the daily sadhana.
  • A chance to participate in the ‘Pancha Bhuta Aradhana’ offering at the Dhyanalinga.
  • A volunteer accompanying the group from Singapore airport and back.
