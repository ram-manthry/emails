You're in for a very special surprise real soon. Please take note of the special announcement at the end of this email.

To volunteer, one doesn't have to do the big things to make a difference; how it is done is what makes all the difference. If reaching out to people comes naturally to you, you could volunteer to make the calls from the comfort of your home. If working on a computer is convenient for you, you could offer to do the data entry work. If there is an organiser in you, you can opt to help with venue setup and in organising other details. There is always something that you can help with based on your time availability, at the same time using this to grow forward in your inner journey.

Sharing from a participant:

"I was told volunteering deepens your experience and takes it to a different level and I wondered how can carrying mats and cutting vegetables impact anything within... but I was willing, willing to do it and see for myself. How small things impacted I did not notice immediately, but it transformed me before I knew it. What happened I still don't know but I am happier, more balanced, focused in whatever role I am playing, whether I am a daughter, sister, wife, mother or banker... I am the best I can be at that moment... much more aware of the inevitability of this moment."

- Geetika, Banker
