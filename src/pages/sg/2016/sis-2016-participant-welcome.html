Guidelines and Useful Information

Please find below some guidelines and useful information. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact the help desk upon arrival and check-in.

Checking-in:

  • Please check-in at the registration desk on Saturday, to receive your registration form, name tag/entry pass.

  • Make sure to wear your name tag at all times during the program, as it is your pass to access the program hall.
  • If you have any physical limitations or any other special needs to be taken into consideration, please update us upon your arrival so we can assist you in the best way possible.

  • Once checked-in, you can access the hall. Please make sure you keep your registration form with you upon entering the hall.
  • Please leave your footwear in the designated area before entering the hall.
  • Please keep your hall entry pass/name tag and hand it over at check in on Sunday (Day 2 of program).

During the program:

  • Seating will be based on your date of registration and category (General/Preferred). Participants requesting for a chair will be assisted by volunteers in the hall.
  • All mobile phones and pagers must be put on silence mode throughout the program.
  • Photographs, videos and audio recordings are not permitted during the program.
  • Please make sure that you are dressed comfortably (loose clothing to be able to sit on the floor). We recommend that you take a shower prior to the program and avoid the use of strong scents such as perfume and cologne, as these could disturb the people sitting next to you.
  • We will provide you with a cushion to use while sitting down.
