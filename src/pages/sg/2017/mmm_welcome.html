Dear Self,

It is my endeavor and privilege to remind you that it is very much
in your grasp to take charge of all that concerns your well-being.
It is time that humanity addresses human well-being in a scientific
manner to take our lives beyond the beliefs that we fight and kill for,
philosophies that we debate and disagree, ideas and ideologies that
divide us, to offer humanity technologies for well-being that we only
have to learn to use.

Be a part of this movement in moving humanity inward
as all human experience is generate from within.
May you know the joy of Being.

I am with you. Let us make it happen,
