Dear Self,

It is my endeavor and privilege to remind you that it is very much in your grasp to take charge of all that concerns your well-being.

Be a part of this movement in moving humanity inward as all human experience is generated from within.

May you know the joy of Being.

I am with you. Let us make it happen,
